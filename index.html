The AuraHome Ecosystem

Three layers.
One seamless safety net.

AuraHome is not a single device — it is a decentralised ecosystem of hardware, AI, and human professionals designed for seniors with no caretaker.

01

Hardware Layer

Guardian Nodes

3–4 discreet nodes — the size of a smoke detector — in the bathroom, bedroom, kitchen, and living room. They use mmWave Radar instead of cameras: radio frequency that sees through steam and darkness, measures heart rate and respiration, and detects falls anywhere in the room.

No images captured. No intrusive cameras. Seniors retain full dignity.

Interaction Layer

The Compass & The Mirror

The Compass — a screen-less wristband with a single button — delivers gentle haptic nudges for medication reminders. One press connects the senior to a live 24/7 human medical concierge, not a robot.

The Mirror Hub handles high-detail tasks: pill recognition, guided physiotherapy, and displaying AI-scheduled doctor appointments on a beautifully legible screen.

Intelligence Layer

Predictive AI & Human-in-the-Loop

The AI builds a "Normal" baseline for each senior. Any deviation triggers a graded response:

  • Gentle check-in: "Hi Mary, you're sleeping in — are you feeling okay today?"
  • Auto-scheduled GP: "Your heart rate has been elevated 48 hrs. Dr. Mehta is free at 2 PM — shall I confirm?"
  • Human Health Manager: On fall or missed check-in, a real person calls first — then dispatches emergency services if unanswered.
